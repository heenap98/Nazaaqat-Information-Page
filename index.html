Peace. Love. Naz.

Started in 2004, Georgia Tech's Nazaaqat is an all-girls classic Bollywood dance team.


GT Nazaaqat's unique style uses classic bollywood songs and dance moves. Because there are no "classic bollywood" competitions we show off our unique style as exhibition acts. As a team, we get to go Raas, Bhangra, Classical and even Fusion competitions. We are able to perform at every competition in the Desi Dance Network and we hope to show you what we are all about this 2019-2020 competition season.
